Support

There are various support from Optimization Services. Besides the following support that OS provides, Optimization Services is also seeking your support. Your support can be in the form as simple as a simple suggestion or comment, or more extensively as development collaborations.

If you feel that that your research and/or current projects share certain aspects that are contibutable to the OS project, please let us know.

If you are building distributed or local optimization and computational systems that can leverage on the Optimization Services framework, we are here to help.

  1. Frequently check this Optimization Services Home Site for most updated information.
  2. Check the OS publication site that includes all the papers, presentations and schedules of OS-related conferences.
  3. Alternatively you can also go to the OS Developer Site hosted at COIN-OR.
  4. Send emails to the OS Mailing List and Standards Mailing List hosted at COIN-OR. The OS developers and all related users should answer your questions in a quick fashion.
  5. You can also go to the OS Newsgroup hosted at Google. Copies of messges posted at the newsgroup are sent to the OS developers.
  6. You can send letters and documents to the following address
    2028 N. Stanton Ct.
    Arlington Heights, IL 60004, U.S.A.
  7. You can directly send emails to support@optimizationservices.org
  8. You can call us at

    (847)275-1779 or (773)702-7456

  9. Search at the OS Developer Site
  10. Go to the OS contact site for more information.


Contact us if you
  • have a question, comment or suggestion
  • want to collaborate on the Optimization Services project
  • contribute certain components to the OS project
  • need help or consulting on bulding OS-compatible software
  • need help or consulting on deploying Optimization Services or integrating Optimization Services in an existing distributed enterprise system
